What is PoliVision?

PoliVision is a political scenario visualisation tool designed to explore how hypothetical vote distributions translate into parliamentary outcomes. It does not predict results or produce commentary. It simply turns assumptions into visual context.

Scenario engine

Explore “what-if” questions

Create coalition and threshold scenarios, compare outcomes, and move quickly between alternatives.

Visual output

Maps, seats, and share

See outcomes as a coherent visual story: regional patterns, seat distribution, and party breakdowns.

Context-first

Designed to avoid “prediction” framing

Outputs are presented as hypothetical scenarios — intended to support analysis, not replace it.

What can you do with it?

Use PoliVision to add structure and visual clarity to your political analysis — without overclaiming certainty.
